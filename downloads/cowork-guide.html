Automations 1–5

The Daily Grind — Handled

1

Email Triage & Smart Replies

Every morning, AI scans your inbox, flags what's urgent, archives the noise, and drafts replies to routine messages — all waiting for your quick approval. No more spending 45 minutes just to feel "caught up."

Example: "Here are 3 emails that need your attention today. I've drafted replies for all of them — just review and send."

2

Invoice Creation & Payment Follow-Up

Say a client's name and the amount — your AI builds a branded invoice with payment links, saves it to your files, and sends it. No template hunting, no formatting. And if they haven't paid in 7 days? It drafts the follow-up automatically.

Example: "Create an invoice for Miami Glow Studio — $1,200 for the UGC package. Done."

3

Social Media Content Drafts

Give your AI a topic, a product, or even just a mood — it produces ready-to-post captions, hooks, and hashtag sets for Instagram, TikTok, and LinkedIn. In seconds. Every day. Without creative block.

Example: "Write 3 Instagram captions for my new fall menu. One funny, one emotional, one direct offer."

5

Meeting Scheduling & Calendar Management

No more back-and-forth emails to find a time. AI checks your calendar, finds open windows, sends scheduling links, and adds confirmed meetings — including location, agenda, and reminders — without you lifting a finger.

Example: "Find a 30-minute slot this week for a call with a new client and send them a booking link."

Automations 6–10

Growth Work — Without the Grind

6

Cold Outreach — Personalized at Scale

Your AI researches a business, drafts a personalized cold email that references their specific situation, and queues it up as a Gmail draft — ready to send in one click. No cookie-cutter blasts. Actual personalization, fast.

Example: "Draft a cold email to Brickell Dental. They're a med spa and I want to pitch my UGC video package."

7

Document & Proposal Creation

Need a client proposal? A service agreement? A one-pager? Describe what you need in plain English and your AI produces a polished, branded document ready to send — in minutes, not hours. No more staring at a blank Word doc.

Example: "Create a 1-page proposal for a 3-month social media retainer — $2,500/month — for a local restaurant client."

8

Follow-Up Email Sequences

After a meeting, a quote, or a new lead — AI drafts the full follow-up sequence. Day 1, Day 3, Day 7. Each one personalized, timed right, and ready to go. Deals close faster when the follow-up never drops.

Example: "Write a 3-part follow-up sequence after a free consultation. Goal is to convert them into a paying client."

9

CRM & Lead Tracker Updates

Instead of manually logging every conversation, AI updates your spreadsheet or CRM: who you contacted, what was said, what the next step is. Your pipeline stays current without you ever opening a spreadsheet voluntarily.

Example: "Log that I spoke with Verde Kitchen today. They're interested. Follow up in 5 days."

10

Content Repurposing Engine

Write one piece of content — a blog post, a video script, a client email — and AI turns it into five things: a caption, a short-form video script, a story, an email, and a Tweet. One idea. Maximum reach. Zero extra time.

Example: "Take this client case study and turn it into an Instagram caption, a LinkedIn post, and an email I can send to my list."

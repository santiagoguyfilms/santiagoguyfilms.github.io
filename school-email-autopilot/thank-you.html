Setup Guide (5 Minutes)

1

Install the Skill in Claude Cowork

Open Claude Cowork on your desktop. Go to Settings → Skills. Drag the school-email-autopilot.skill file into the window, or click "Add Skill" and browse to the file.

Don't have Claude Cowork yet? Download it at claude.ai — it's Anthropic's desktop AI assistant.
2

Connect Your Gmail, Google Calendar, and Slack

In Cowork settings, connect these three free MCP connectors:

Gmail — so the skill can read your school emails
Google Calendar — so it can add events and deadlines
Slack — so it can post your daily digest (share the channel with family!)

Each connector takes about 60 seconds to set up. Just click "Connect" and authorize with your Google/Slack account.
3

Run It and Enter Your School's Domain

In Cowork, just say: "Run the school email digest"

The first time you run it, the skill will ask for your school's email domain — something like @lincolnelementary.org or @miamidadeschools.net. Enter it, and the skill configures everything automatically.

After the first run, you can schedule it to run automatically every morning. Just say: "Schedule the school digest to run every day at 7am."
4

Share With Your Family (Optional)

Invite your co-parent, nanny, or grandparents to the Slack channel. Everyone gets the same daily digest — no more forwarding emails or texting "did you see the school email about..."
